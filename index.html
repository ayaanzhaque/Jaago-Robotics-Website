Jaago Robotics

Partnered with the Jaago Foundation to Provide STEM and Robotics Education to the Brightest Students in Bangladesh.

What We do

Providing Robotics Education

We teach Robotics, Coding, and STEM to underpriveledged students in Bangladesh at the Jaago Foundation. As a branch of the Jaago Foundation, we use the Lego Mindstorms EV3 Robotics kit to help young students learn introductory robotics concepts. We want to provide new opportunities for students who don't have access to them.

About The Program

Our goal is to expand Robotics and Coding in Bangladesh.

We go once every year to teach Robotics and Coding to the bright students at the Jaago Schoool in Dhaka. We have gone twice so far, in the summer of 2018 and winter of 2019.

Promoting Robotics and Skills

We aim to teach the fundamentals of Robotics and Coding to students who have no exposure. As a branch of Jaago, we want to teach motivated and inspired students who want to have new opportunites in often unaccessible fields, such as Computer Science and Engineering.
