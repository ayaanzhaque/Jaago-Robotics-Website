Jaago Robotics

Partnered with the Jaago Foundation to Provide STEM and Robotics Education to the Brightest Students in Bangladesh.

What We do

Providing STEM Education

We teach Robotics, Coding, and STEM to underpriveledged students in Bangladesh at the Jaago Foundation. We use the Lego Mindstorms EV3 Robotics kit to help young students learn introductory robotics concepts. We want to provide new opportunities for students who don't have access to them.
